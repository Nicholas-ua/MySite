Short about me

Hello! I am Mykola Savych. I want to tell you about some CAD-systems. Every time we need new knowledge about world to create new things. CAD-systems help us to create high-technology things for save resources. I use CAD-systems to create, calculates and writes technical documentations for woodworking machines and components. With CAD-systems we can discovers designing woodworking tools for different woodworking machines and jobs. On my website I want to demonstrate some created elements of projects. These projects has been created by various CAD-systems: KOMPAS 3D, SOLIDWORKS, INVENTOR. You can look at some of these elements and make your own mind about this. May be someone who wants to be engineer and make beautiful things will want first to study and then to use such as these systems. I hope my website will be helpful for other people too.
